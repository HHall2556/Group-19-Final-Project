Project Clean Air

Hello, welcome to Project Clean Air's website. Our project is dedicated to spreading awareness of pollution and helping concerned citizens like yourself find resources to lend a helping hand. You can navigate to our informational data pages from the left bar, and navigate the rest of the website on top. Our data visualizations include a barchart showing the average pollutants in each state. Furthermore, we have maps of the United States to show you a geographical interpretation of all reported states and their pollutant concentrations.
